MUSE 61st Pig Roast

June 20-23

Join us for the 61st Annual Muse Pig Roast on June 22nd, a day of delicious food, cherished connections, and unforgettable memories. Whether active or retired MUSE tech, our bond as the Muse family is profound. It's not just about the roast pig; it's about sharing stories, laughter, and the camaraderie that makes our community unique. Come hungry, come happy, and come prepared for a fantastic celebration of our special bond.
